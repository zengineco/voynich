## voynich_paper.docx

**Superseded by preprint v1.0.** Known errors retained for the record:

- The f30v "pure blue borage control test" claim is wrong. f30v shows chor at 6+ occurrences and high ot- density. The borage control test as originally framed failed.
- The f28r line-by-line glyph readings (crod line 1, croe line 2, etc.) are not present in ZL3b. They derive from a visual image-reading session in which glyph-reading accuracy was acknowledged as unreliable. What ZL3b actually shows on f28r: otchor × 2, chol × 5, cthol × multiple.
- Frequency counts in the original abstract are inflated. Earlier draft cited chor family = 254 and chol family = 520; ZL3b counts under v4 tokenisation are chor = 199 and chol = 381 (or 198 and 344 under an alternative comma-no-split tokenisation).
- The "-ar 1.82× concentration in Block 2 versus Block 1" figure is unverified and should be treated as an unsupported hypothesis until paragraph-position analysis is run on ZL3b.
- The "ibn Tibbon *poael*" parallel cites the existence of Freudenthal (2016) correctly but the specific identification of the cited glossary as the "surviving philosophical glossary" has not been verified against the Freudenthal text.

The preprint v1.0 makes no claims dependent on any of the above.

## Drift points that should NEVER be repeated, anywhere

These are claims that were once made in the project record but have been verified wrong against ZL3b. They are listed here so future-self and any reviewer can confirm they are not accidentally revived.

- **"chor = red colour."** Dead. chor is a paradigmatic root that pairs with chol at r = 0.99 across 26 prefix-wrappers. They are word-class siblings, not opposed colour or quality terms.
- **"f30v shows zero chor."** False. f30v shows 6+ chor.
- **"f28r shows six distinct cr- forms at specified line positions."** Not in ZL3b under any tokenisation.
- **"chedy is the Voynich analog of Avicennan hot/cold/dry/moist."** Category error. chedy is an inflectional binyan, not a lexical item.
- **"The Voynich is an abridgement of the Canon of Medicine."** Not supported. The preprint reports three of five structural-comparison tests matching, but explicitly disclaims textual derivation.
- **"ibn Tibbon Montpellier authorship."** Not supported by any test reported in the preprint. May remain as a historical-context speculation in private notes; must not be asserted in any public document.
- **"otch- / otal- pharmacy lock."** Speculative pattern claim from early sessions; never verified.
- **"f102v / f103r Avicennan handshake."** Speculative; never verified.
- **"8aug is a member of the [Cdy] ablaut."** Wrong. 8aug is a conjunction. The ablaut system operates on ch-d-y skeletons, not on 8-prefix tokens.
- **Inflated frequency counts**: where the project record cites -ar = 427, chor family = 459, chol family = 691, these were earlier-tokeniser figures and do not match v4 counts. Use the preprint figures.
