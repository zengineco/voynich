## §4. THE 2-HUMOR FOREGROUNDING MECHANISM (LOCKED — major 15 May finding)

This is the cleanest substantive discovery of the session.

### The pattern

Each herbal-A folio FOREGROUNDS one humor base (CH or SH) and BACKGROUNDS the other:

| Group | CH-base bare% | CH prefix-dec% | CH o-grade% | SH-base bare% | SH prefix-dec% | SH o-grade% |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| SH-dom folios | **42.1** | 35.2 | 37.1 | 21.7 | **19.1** | **46.1** |
| CH-strong folios | 26.8 | **41.4** | **56.6** | 28.9 | 13.2 | 47.9 |

**SH-dom folios:** SH-base gets heavy decoration + o-grade (foreground); CH-base gets bare forms (background)
**CH-strong folios:** CH-base gets heavy decoration + o-grade (foreground); SH-base gets less

### Folio specialization (T16 inverted)

65 herbal-A folios with ≥5 humor-pair tokens:
- 5 SH-dominant (CH:SH < 1.0): f23v, f29r, f42r, f44v, f46r
- 15 CH-strongly-dominant (CH:SH > 5.0): f17v (12.0), f56r, f2v, f6v, f20r, f23r, f29v, f32r, f3r, f32v, f13r, f15v, f24v, f52v, f21r
- 45 mixed
- Mean CH:SH = 3.93, stdev 2.83, range 0.20 → 12.00

**Folios are NOT uniform** — they specialize. Consistent with each folio describing one substance with primary humor classification.

### Within-humor grade specialization

For folios with both chor ≥2 and chol ≥2:
- chor-dominant (chor:chol > 2): f6v, f3v, f8r
- chol-dominant (chor:chol < 0.5): f47r, f1r, f17v, f21r, f27r

**A 2×2 prediction grid emerges:**
- CH-humor + chor-grade: f6v, f3v, f8r
- CH-humor + chol-grade: f47r, f1r, f17v, f21r, f27r, f23r
- SH-humor + shor-grade: smaller absolute counts; needs T16 follow-up
- SH-humor + shol-grade: f23v, f29r, f42r, f44v, f46r tend here
